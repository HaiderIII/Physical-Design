## How to Update

After completing a puzzle:

1. Update the Statistics table (increment Completed count)
2. Check off the Achievement if applicable
3. Add an entry to the Puzzle Log

Example:
```markdown
| 2024-01-15 | syn_001 | 18 min | Library path was wrong |
```
